How It Works

In app.py, we pass multiple variables:

return render_template('profile.html',
    name='Shreya Jape',
    age=22,
    course='Web Development',
    email='shreya@example.com',
    city='Pune',
    is_enrolled=True,
    github='shreya-jape',
    linkedin='shreya-jape')
